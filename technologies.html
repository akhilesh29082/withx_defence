WITHX Drone Airbag Recovery System

Automatic Water-Activated Drone Recovery Technology

The WITHX Drone Airbag Recovery System protects expensive drone hardware and payloads during accidental water landings. By deploying high-buoyancy flotation supports instantly upon water detection, it ensures that valuable assets do not sink.

Core Features

  • Water-Activated Deployment (<120ms latency)
  • Automatic High-Buoyancy Flotation Support
  • Ultra-Lightweight Carbon Gas Shell Assembly
  • Zero Passive Electrical Power Required (Sensing Mode)
  • High-Frequency Water Conductivity Array Sensors
  • Modular Fit For Multi-Rotor & Fixed-Wing Units

Primary Applications

Marine Drone Operations Water Surveillance Missions Coastal Monitoring Rescue Operations Commercial & Research Drones

System Benefits

  • Reduces Risk of Drone & Payload Loss
  • Improves Recovery Success Rate in open oceans
  • Enhances Operational Safety near crowded waterways
  • Supports Water-Based Research Missions & Sensor Drops

System Specifications

System Dry Weight 220g (Excluding gas canister)
Inflation Speed 120ms (CO2 cold-gas cartridge)
Flotation Buoyancy Supports up to 25kg Payload
Sensing Array Quad Gold-Plated Electro-Resistive

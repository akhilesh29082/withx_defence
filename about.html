Our Mission

Engineering Intelligent Unmanned Systems

WITHX Defence is committed to bridging the gap between theoretical autonomy and practical field survival.

Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) perform critical operations globally, but remain vulnerable to mechanical hazards and environmental threats. We develop active, intelligent protection solutions alongside highly responsive sensory arrays.

By aligning next-generation edge machine learning with responsive mechanical hardware (such as water-activated flotation bladders), we seek to minimize equipment loss, maximize reconnaissance timeframes, and protect operational assets.

Core Values

Design for Extremes

Our systems are engineered to withstand rough landings, ocean spray, and high wind shear.

Redundancy by Default

Every deployment mechanism is backed by mechanical failsafes to guarantee operation.

Rapid Hardware Loops

Continuous simulation testing, water tank drops, and wind tunnel calibration iterations.

Frequently Asked Questions

System Deploy & Procurement

What drone platforms are compatible with the Airbag Recovery System?

The WITHX Airbag Recovery System features a modular bracket assembly. It is compatible with DJI Enterprise series (M300/M350 RTK), Custom Carbon Quadcopters/Hexacopters with standard rail installations, and medium-scale fixed-wing surveying platforms.

How does the water-activated deployment trigger without power?

The sensors trigger gas canisters using mechanical hydro-valves paired with high-frequency resistive grids. If the main battery fails during a water landing, the chemical backup cell maintains sufficient electrical potential to ignite the micro gas-generator instant-inflation mechanism automatically.

Is the surveillance system compliant with export control regulations?

Yes, our surveillance systems are designed to meet ITAR compliance guidelines for electro-optical sensors. Procurement queries require organizational verification prior to hardware disclosure.
